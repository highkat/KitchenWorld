2D floor plan

Dimensioned plan showing cabinet runs, wall types, plumbing rough-ins, and appliance placement to 1/4 scale.

02 · ELEVATIONS

Elevations

Our design process

A five-phase engagement.

From the first showroom walk-through to post-install punch list. Typical kitchen project spans 10–18 weeks end to end.

01WK 1

Discovery & showroom visit

Free 60-minute showroom appointment. We talk budget, scope, timeline, and who's doing the work. You walk the floor; we flag cabinet lines worth considering.

FREE · 60 MINPHILADELPHIA · FURLONG · BRIDGEPORT
02WK 2

On-site measure

A designer and estimator visit your home. Field measurements, photos, plumbing/electrical notes, structural check. 90 minutes typical.

FREE · 90 MININCLUDES WRITTEN NOTES
03WK 3–4

Design presentation

Back in the showroom: 2D plan, elevations, and live 3D walkthrough. We make changes in real time. Plan on 2–3 rounds before final.

$500 RETAINER · APPLIED TO ORDER
04WK 5

Final spec & order

Signed drawing set, itemized spec sheet, payment schedule. Cabinets go into production; countertop slab gets reserved at the yard.

50% DEPOSIT · LEAD TIME BEGINS
05WK 12+

Delivery & install

Cabinets stage at our yard, get inspected, ship to site. Installers set and scribe over 3–5 days. Countertop templated after install; final surfaces follow in 2 weeks.

INSTALL + COUNTERTOP + PUNCH LIST
