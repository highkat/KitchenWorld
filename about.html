Our story

What we do, why we still do it this way.

Kitchen World opened in 1987 as a single Philadelphia showroom specifying kitchen cabinetry for local homeowners and general contractors. We've added two more showrooms, picked up another generation of designers, and represented dozens of cabinet lines — but the core hasn't changed.

We're a cabinetry dealer, not a general contractor. That distinction matters. Our job is to spec the boxes, doors, drawers, and surfaces — and to get them to your site in perfect condition, on time, with drawings your installer can actually build from. We stay out of your plumbing, drywall, and paint. We're good at the thing we're good at.

"We pride ourselves on customer service — before, during, and after the sale."

The "wholesale" in our model is literal: we buy from manufacturers at trade prices and pass those prices to homeowners and contractors alike. No retail markup means we win business the same way we did in 1987 — on specification quality, designer expertise, and install follow-through.

Three generations of Philadelphia families have now furnished their kitchens with cabinets we sold them. We know because we still hear from their kids.
