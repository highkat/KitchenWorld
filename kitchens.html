How we work

From consult to coffee on the new island.

Four phases, one point of contact, no handoffs. Expect 10–18 weeks from first appointment to install, depending on cabinet line.

01

Consult & measure

A designer meets you in the showroom or at home. We measure, photograph, and note every obstacle — vents, plumbing, electrical, structural.

02

Design & specify

2D plans, elevations, and a full 3D walkthrough. We finalize door style, finish, hardware, and trim before a dollar is committed.

03

Quote & order

Line-by-line itemized pricing. Once you approve, we release the cabinet order and lock the install window with our crew.

04

Deliver & install

Cabinets stage at our yard, get inspected, and ship to site. Our installers set, scribe, and punch-list within 3–5 days for a typical kitchen.

What's included

PLYWOOD CONSTRUCTION

Full 3/4" plywood boxes and backs standard across every mid- and upper-tier line we represent.

SOFT-CLOSE HINGES

Concealed 6-way adjustable soft-close hinges on every door. No optional upgrade line.

Filler strips are scribed to walls on site by our installers. No caulk gaps, no visible seams.
