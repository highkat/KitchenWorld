Common questions

Before you reach out.

The questions we hear most often from first-time clients.

Is the first consultation really free?

Yes. A 60-minute showroom appointment costs nothing. We only charge a design retainer ($500) if you ask us to produce a full drawing set — and it's applied to your cabinet order.

Do I need to bring plans?

Helpful but not required. Plans, photos, rough sketches, or even just a few Pinterest boards all work. We can measure on a follow-up site visit.

How long does a kitchen take?

10–18 weeks from first consult to install, depending on cabinet line. Stock lines run fast; custom lines add 4–6 weeks of lead time.

Do you work with contractors?

Constantly. We have a full trade program — designer discounts, net-30 terms, shop drawings GCs can actually build from.

Do you install, or just sell cabinets?

Both. We have an in-house install crew for customers who want it turnkey, and we'll happily spec cabinets for you to hand to your own GC.

What brands do you represent?

40+ cabinet lines across custom, semi-custom, and stock tiers — plus quartz, granite, and laminate from every major surface manufacturer.
