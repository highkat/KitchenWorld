Fabrication process

From slab to installed edge.

Every countertop we fabricate runs the same five-step process. Most jobs install 2–3 weeks after the template.

STEP 01

Slab selection

Pick your slab on-site at our Philadelphia yard. Full slabs out, natural light, no photos-of-photos.

STEP 02

Digital template

Laser-measured after cabinets are set. We capture every outlet, faucet, and wall flex to 1/16".

STEP 03

Shop fabrication

CNC-cut, edge-profiled, polished, and sink-cutouts fitted. You can stop by and inspect before it ships.

STEP 04

Site install

Our crew delivers, sets, and seams on the same day. Disconnect & reconnect of plumbing coordinated.

STEP 05

Final seal

Stone gets sealed before we leave. Care & maintenance sheet goes in the drawer.
